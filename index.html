Impact Labs

Inspiring and empowering ambitious computer scientists to build a better world.

What is Impact Labs?

Impact Labs is an organization that provides greater access to opportunities at the intersection of technology and social good. We connect students to seasoned social entrepreneurs, meaningful career opportunities, and capital to help ignite their ideas. This is accomplished through a suite of initiatives, detailed below.




The Fellowship

The Impact Fellowship is a 2 week program for talented computer science students who are passionate about using their tech skills to make an impact. Fellows work in teams to create their own projects that address the UN Sustainable Development Goals, studying with amazing mentors, taking advanced software engineering workshops, meeting top leaders in the social impact space, and pitching their ideas at the culmination of the program for the chance to win funding. Applications for 2019 are open now!
