What is Impact Labs?

Impact Labs is an organization that provides greater access to opportunities at the intersection of technology and social good. We connect students to seasoned social entrepreneurs, meaningful career opportunities, and capital to help ignite their ideas. This is accomplished through a suite of initiatives, detailed below.

The Fellowship

The Impact Fellowship is a 2 week program for talented computer science students who are passionate about using their tech skills to make an impact. Fellows work in teams to create their own projects that address the UN Sustainable Development Goals, studying with amazing mentors, taking advanced software engineering workshops, meeting top leaders in the social impact space, and pitching their ideas at the culmination of the program for the chance to win funding.

The Summit

The Impact Summit is a weekend-long conference for tech students to explore how they can leverage their skills for the common good. The first Impact Summit took place in June of 2018 in NYC with 250 CS students from all over the world, over 60 speakers,a career expo, a blockchain hackathon, and a social startup competition with over $30,000 in prize money awarded. Check out some of the speakers from the summit!

The Coalition

One of our primary goals at Impact Labs is to match the most enthusiastic computer scientists with the companies that are doing the most good. The Impact Coalition is a group of social startups, high-impact companies, and NGOs that are actively seeking interns and full-time engineers beginning in the summer of 2019. It's the Common App for social good. One application, over 30 participating companies! Applications are open now!
