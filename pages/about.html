Our Story

Founded in 2017, Impact Labs started in a Brown University dorm room with two bright-eyed visionaries frustrated by the fact that talented computer science students wanted to use their skills to make meaningful social impact, yet they often ended up flocking to Silicon Valley giants post-graduation.

Impact Labs was born to empower individuals to find purposeful, fulfilling technology careers in social impact. Since our inception, we have hosted hundreds of attendees across our 5 in-person and virtual Impact Summits, 150 fellows as part of our four Impact Fellowships, and built up a network of ~5,000 technologists, partners, and sponsors.

Our Core Values

Intentionality

We value intentionality and thoughtfulness in everything we do. Social impact is multifaceted and complex, and we strive to understand its nuances, respect its different forms, and recognize the structural challenges in place that make progress nonlinear.

Empathy

Dreaming Big

We love to dream beyond conventional norms. Our hope is to open the doors of possibility for young change-makers who are eager to leverage their skillsets in meaningful careers that match their interests and values. Building platforms for high-risk refugee populations. Developing software solutions to improve voter turnout. Engineering new solar panel distribution systems. The possibilities are endless!
