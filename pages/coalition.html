About the Coalition

We’re here to simplify the process of landing your next dream career. We find open engineering, design and product roles at incredible startups, nonprofits, and organizations leveraging technology to build a better world, then we match you with opportunities where you'd make the best fit!

Apply once to be considered for dozens of opportunities!

Think of it like a Common Application for socially impactful technology careers.

Apply once to be considered for numerous opportunities within impact areas like climate and sustainability, racial equity, poverty alleviation, and more.

How It Works

Submit Your Application

This program is designed for those seeking engineering, product management, or design roles. That being said, anyone is welcome to apply!

Get Matched!

We’ll introduce you to companies we think you'd make a great fit for! You'll then have a chance to move forward with your specific company matches.

  Impact Coalition FAQs




Who can apply?

Anyone experienced in engineering, product, or design looking for positions at high-impact organizations.


How long should I expect this process to take?

You can expect the first round of matches to take place within 1-2 months. However, we will continue to recommend qualified applicants to companies on a rolling basis as long as there are open positions. We are also continuously partnering with new companies, so unmatched applicants will still be under consideration for new opportunities as they arise.


What happens if I don’t get a match?

Unfortunately, we can’t guarantee that you’ll be matched. If you’ve applied and made it through our interview processes, we’ll keep your info on file for the next application cycle (no need to re-interview) and automatically let you know if future roles seem like a great match! Join our newsletter to hear about any future application cycles.


Are there open spots for companies interested in joining?

Yes! If your company or nonprofit is interested in joining (or you’d like to nominate an organization that you think would be interested in joining), reach out to hello@impactlabs.io with the subject line “Coalition Company” and we’ll get back to you as soon as we can!



How do we make matches?

We’ll take into consideration your preferences, skillsets and interests, as well as the company’s needs, to find you the most compatible opportunities. It’s important that you fill out the application as accurately as possible so we can make well-informed matches!


I got matched, now what?

Congrats! Once we e-introduce you to a matched company, it’ll be up to you and the company representative to maintain communication about next steps. Each company will have its own internal hiring process you can expect to undergo, and if all goes well then (fingers crossed!) you’ll receive an offer to work at an impactful, mission-driven company.

Feedback is super important to us, so we’ll be checking in after you get matched to see how things are going.


Help! I’m still confused! Where can I ask more questions?

We’ve got you covered! Reach out to hello@impactlabs.io and we’ll respond as quickly as we can :)
