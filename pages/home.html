What is Impact Labs?

Impact Labs is an organization that provides greater access to opportunities at the intersection of technology and social good. We connect students to seasoned social entrepreneurs, meaningful career opportunities, and capital to help ignite their ideas.

This is accomplished through a suite of initiatives, detailed below.

The Impact Fellowship

The Impact Fellowship is a 2 week program for talented computer science students who are passionate about using their tech skills to make an impact.

Fellows take advanced software engineering workshops, study with amazing mentors, meet top leaders in the social impact space, and work in teams to create their own projects that address the UN Sustainable Development Goals.


Learn More

The Impact Summit

The Impact Summit unites students who are passionate about technology and social impact, while connecting them with social entrepreneurs, civic activists, and thought leaders who are leveraging technology for the common good.

The next Impact Summit will be taking place online this August!


Learn More

The Impact Coalition

The Impact Coalition matches talented and enthusiastic computer scientists with high-impact startups, mid-sized social enterprises, and NGOs that are actively seeking interns and full time engineers. Think of it as the Common App for social startups: One application - over 30 participating organizations.
