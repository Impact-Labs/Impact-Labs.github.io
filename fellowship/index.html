The Impact Fellowship

A 2-week technical fellowship for the next generation of social entrepreneurs. January 2nd - January 16th, 2019 New York City

About the Fellowship

The Impact Fellowship is a gathering of talented and passionate computer science students who want to use their skills for social good!

Mentorship

Fellows will learn advanced software engineering from top bootcamp instructors and industry leaders, with courses in JavaScript (React, Node.js), DevOps and DBs (AWS, MongoDB, Firebase), Smart Contracts (Solidity), and Machine Learning(PyTorch).

Speakers

The fellowship will feature amazing startup founders and nonprofit leaders who are leveraging technology to tackle the most urgent challenges of our generation, such as climate change, healthcare inadequacies, global poverty, gender inequalities, and lack of access to education.

Experience

Students spend a week launching a tech-driven social venture that they pitch to a panel of entrepreneurial judges. Through hands-on mentorship and social entrepreneurship workshops, students can leave with significant progress on a semester project, team hustle, or serious social venture.

Community

More important than the skills learned during the fellowship are the incredible people we get meet along the way. The Impact Fellowship creates a community of like-minded, dedicated engineers who hope to build a more sustainable, equitable world.
